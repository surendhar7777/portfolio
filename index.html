About

I'm an MSc student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at the University of Hull, passionate about applying cutting-edge AI to solve real-world problems — particularly in predictive modeling, computer vision, and spatio-temporal analysis.

My work spans the full ML pipeline: from raw data wrangling and feature engineering through to deploying production-ready models. I'm especially drawn to problems where the stakes are high — detecting disease early, predicting crime, and making cities safer.

Outside of academia, I bring a leadership and operations mindset honed through team management — I know how to execute, not just theorise.
